Complete AI Tool Guide

Text‑to‑Image (Stable Diffusion)

Our text‑to‑image tool is powered by Stable Diffusion 3.5. It generates 1024x1024 images from text prompts. Parameters:

  • prompt – the description (required)
  • negative_prompt – what to avoid (e.g., “blurry, ugly”)
  • seed – number for reproducible results
  • steps – 20–50, more steps = better quality
  • cfg_scale – 7–12, how closely to follow the prompt

Example: /imagine prompt: a cyberpunk cat, neon lights, rainy street, 4k --neg blurry, ugly --steps 30 --cfg 9

Image‑to‑Video (Stable Video Diffusion)

Generates 4‑second MP4 videos from a single image. Additional options:

  • motion_bucket_id – 0–255, controls motion intensity
  • decoding_t – 1–8, frames per second
  • seed – for consistency

Example: /video image: [upload] prompt: ocean waves --motion 200 --fps 6

Motion Control (AnimateDiff + ControlNet)

This tool animates images with precise motion control. Use:

  • path – define movement (e.g., “circle”, “left to right”)
  • speed – slow, medium, fast
  • style – natural, robotic, fluid

Example: /motion image: [upload] path: spiral speed: slow style: fluid

Getting Help

If you’re stuck, use the /help command in any channel to see available commands. For technical issues, reach out in the #support channel on Discord.
